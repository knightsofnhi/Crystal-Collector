Crystal Collector

Rules of the Game

  1. There are four gems below.
    • Opal
    • Sapphire
    • Amethyst
    • Diamond
  2. Add a specific amount of points to your total score by clicking on the gems.
  3. Match your total score to the random number.
  4. If your score goes above the random number, then you lose.
  5. The value of each gem is hidden until you click on it.
  6. Each new game will change the values of the gems.
